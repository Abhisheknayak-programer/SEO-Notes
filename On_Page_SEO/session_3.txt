TYPES OF SEO
1. On Page SEO
2. OFF Page SEO

ON PAGE SEO
On page seo is the process of optimizing elements on your website itself.
Ex: Header tag, meta descriptions, title tags, keywords research, keyword usage, internal links, site maps.

KEYWORD RESEARCH
> It is the most important activity of the SEO.
> It is a practice where you choose the primary and secondary keywords around which meta tags and content will be based on.
> The primary component to keyword research are : 
1. Search volumes
2. Competitions
3. Relevancy
NOTE : Always select More Search Voulme and less Competitions.

The Tool used for the keyword research : Google Keyword Planner
Link : https://www.wordstream.com/keywords
